Editor's note

The creators of this website, Asees Kaur and Anishka Goel are besties studing in grade 7 of The Heritage School. They think that the now digitized world needs more platforms to do day-to-day tasks such as reading.


This website has been made in order to help young readers pursue their passion and get people to fall in love with the endless world of books. The creators themselves love to read and have tirelessly hand-picked this large collection of books for people to enjoy. We hope you find books you are looking for.
